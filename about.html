About Refoodify

We're on a mission to eliminate food waste through technology, education, and community empowerment.

Building a Zero-Waste Future

Founded in 2025, Refoodify emerged from a simple observation: billions of pounds of food are wasted annually while millions face food insecurity. We build tools, provide education, and connect communities to reduce food waste and create positive environmental impact.

Today, we're proud to serve a global community of individuals, restaurants, and organizations committed to reducing food waste and creating measurable impact.
